Cuphead

Cuphead tells us the story of Cuphead and Mugman, a couple of brothers who made an ambitious bet with the devil, which they ended up losing. As a consequence, Cuphead and Mugman have to go over the world killing all the people that were in debt with the devil. Fortunately, this story has some chance to have a good ending... The 1930's cartoon-inspired art is the key feature that makes of Cuphead unique, not to also mention its mechanics and shooting style.
